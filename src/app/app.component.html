About Us
SacredPrayer.Org is an initiative of ISKCON Online.
On one side there is a lot of good things happening in the world now, in terms of technology and advancement. On the other hand there are a lot of new age problems such as depression, intolerance, addictions, becoming rampent even at a early age of a child. Can we make a better future? Yes! we can. We at SacredPrayer.Org wish to work towards assisting each and everyone of you to become happy! That's our introduction.
Our Gratitude
Our deepest gratitude to His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupda, for the showing us the path of eternal happiness by his illuminating teachings. Srila Prabhupada has translated many of the Vedic teachings into english, which is now available in all most all the languages. Like to get one, contact us.
